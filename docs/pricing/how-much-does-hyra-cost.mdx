# How much does Hyra cost?

Currently, Hyra offers **one** plan on its self-service platform. This plan includes every feature Hyra has to offer.

## Pricing

Hyra 'Premium' is available in localised pricing for your country.

<Tabs>
    <TabItem value="USD" label="United States" default>
        <p>In the United States, customers are charged <b>$5.99</b> per month.</p>
        <p>Any applicable taxes are included in the price</p>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem value="GBP" label="United Kingdom">
        <p>In the United Kingdom, customers are charged <b>£5.99</b> per month.</p>
        <p>VAT at 20% is included in the price</p>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem value="AUD" label="Australia">
        <p>In Australia, customers are charged <b>A$10.99</b> per month.</p>
        <p>Any applicable taxes are included in the price</p>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem value="CAD" label="Canada">
        <p>In Canada, customers are charged <b>CA$8.59</b> per month.</p>
        <p>Any applicable taxes are included in the price</p>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem value="EUR" label="Euro">
        <p><b>Any country using the Euro as their primary currency will be charged in Euros</b>. If your country is in Europe, but does not use Euros as the primary currency, for example: Sweden, Bulgaria or Switzerland, please see 'Rest of World'</p>
        <p>Countries using Euros will be charged <b>€5.99</b> per month.</p>
        <p>Any tax for your relevant teritory is included in the price</p>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem value="RoW" label="Rest of World">
        <p>Countries which do not fall within the other tabs are charged our 'Rest of World' price. Currently, our Rest of World price mirrors our United States pricing.</p>
        <p>You will be charged <b>$5.99</b> per month.</p>
        <p>Any teritories which Hyra is required to pay any tax in will have tax included in the price.</p>
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Annual Discounts

Hyra does not currently offer any annual pricing. We are actively looking into this option.

## A note about Account Managers

If your group has been assigned an account manager, pricing may differ from the prices stated on this page. For a full breakdown of your pricing, please contact your account manager.
